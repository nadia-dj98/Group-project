Atlantics

Rating: 6.7/10

star star star grade grade


In a popular suburb of Dakar, workers on the construction site of a futuristic tower, without pay for months, decide to leave the country by the ocean for a better future. Among them is Souleiman, the lover of Ada, promised to another.

Genre: Drama, Romance, Mystery
Directed by: Mati Diop
Screenplay by: Mati Diop, Olivier Demangel
Starring: Ibrahima Traoré, Mame Bineta Sane, Amadou Mbow, Nicole Sougou, Aminata Kane, Mariama Gassama
Produced by: Judith Lou Lévy, Eve Robin
Realeased: 2019
Duration: 104 min
Countries: France, Senegal, Belgium
Languages: Wolof, French
