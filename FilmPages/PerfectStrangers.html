Perfect Strangers

Rating: 7.7/10

star star star star grade


Seven close friends (three couples and a bachelor) gather for an intimate dinner party. After an incoming phone call disrupts the dinner party, its members unanimously agree on placing their phones on the table and allowing the others to scrutinise their private affairs.

Genre: Comedy, Drama
Directed by: Paolo Genovese
Screenplay by: Filippo Bologna, Paolo Costella, Paolo Genovese, Paola Mammini, Rolando Ravello
Starring: Giuseppe Battiston, Anna Foglietta, Marco Giallini, Edoardo Leo, Valerio Mastandrea, Alba Rohrwacher, Kasia Smutniak
Produced by: Marco Belardi
Realeased: 2016
Duration: 97 min
Country: Italy
Language: Italian
